1) Purpose of the Study

This study explores how people interact with online news articles and what influences readers' trust, perception, and decision-making.

2) What Will You Be Asked to Do?

You will be asked to read three news articles, select recommended follow-ups, and answer a few short questionnaires. The study takes approximately 10 minutes. There are no known risks or discomforts involved.

3) Data Privacy and Your Rights
  • Your participation is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time without giving a reason.
  • All personal data will be kept confidential and used only for the purposes of this study. Further processing of anonymized data may occur if it aligns with the study's goals. Any data shared with other researchers or published will be fully anonymized and untraceable to you.
